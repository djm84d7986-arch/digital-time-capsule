My Memories

I remember logging onto AIM after school. Hearing that dial-up tone always brings me back. Everyone had their own weird screen names and pixel hearts next to their crush's name.

Back then, websites felt personal — like each one had its own story. There wasn’t a “like” button, just creativity and self-expression.

It’s funny how the old web looked messy but felt more real than anything now.
